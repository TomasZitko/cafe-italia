A Passion for Italy

Our Story

Founded on a love for authentic Italian hospitality, Cafe Italia is more than just a place to dine—it's an experience. Nestled in the heart of Prague, we bring a slice of Italy to you, with recipes passed down through generations and an ambiance that feels like home. Our commitment is to quality, from the carefully selected coffee beans to the freshest ingredients in our kitchen.
